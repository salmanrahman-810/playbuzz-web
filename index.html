The playbuzz

Play Buzz is more than just a cricket group—it's a community of passionate players and fans who live and breathe the game! Whether you're an aspiring cricketer, a weekend warrior, or just someone who loves discussing the latest matches and stats, Play Buzz is the place for you. We organize friendly matches, tournaments, and practice sessions to keep the cricket fever alive. Our group is all about teamwork, sportsmanship, and, most importantly, the joy of playing cricket. From gully cricket to competitive league games, we welcome players of all skill levels. Join Play Buzz and be part of the excitement! Let’s hit those sixes, take stunning catches, and celebrate the spirit of cricket together! 🏏🔥


About us

Founded on December 6, 2023, PlayBuzz Galaxy Gladiators Mymensingh is a cricket group built for passionate players who love the game and the spirit of friendly competition. We come together once a week on weekends to play exciting and energetic friendly matches, fostering teamwork, sportsmanship, and fun. Whether you're a seasoned player or just looking to enjoy a good game with like-minded cricket lovers, our team is all about passion, dedication, and making every match a memorable one. Join us and be a part of the Galaxy Gladiators as we hit the field and play the game we love! Let me know if you’d like any modifications!
